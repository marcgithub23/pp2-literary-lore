Introduction

Hello there, fellow literary enthusiast! Do you know your literary lore well? Care to test your knowledge and answer ten trivia questions?

Here are the rules:

  • Enter a username.
  • Select a game category based on fiction titles (Alice in Wonderland, Harry Potter, and Mistborn). The ten questions will be about the title you select.
  • Select a level of difficulty and the timer will be set accordingly. You will have 7 minutes and 30 seconds to complete and answer all the questions on the Easy level, 5 minutes on Normal, and 2 minutes and 30 seconds on Hard.
  • Once you click start, you will immediately receive feedback as to whether the answer youâ€™ve selected is the correct or wrong one. Click on the next button to proceed to the next question. The timer and your progress will be shown above the questions. You may also click on the quit button at any point to go back to the home screen.
  • Once you complete the quiz, you will see a tally of your total score. You can click on the try again button to retake the same quiz with the same game category and level of difficulty, but the questions and answers will be shuffled to keep it challenging! Otherwise, you can click on the home button to go back to the home screen and try a different game category and/or level of difficulty.
